## FAQ Section

### Why is ScreamingCAT free?

ScreamingCAT is an open-source project built by developers who believe professional SEO tools should be accessible to everyone — not just teams with enterprise budgets.

### Is there a paid version with more features?

No. There is one version, and it includes everything. We don't gate features behind a paywall.

### Are there crawl limits?

No. You can crawl as many URLs as your machine can handle. There is no 500-URL cap or license-based restriction.

### How does ScreamingCAT compare to paid crawlers?

ScreamingCAT offers comparable depth of analysis to tools like Screaming Frog SEO Spider, Sitebulb, and DeepCrawl — including JavaScript rendering, Google integrations, and 60+ issue types. The main difference: it costs nothing.

### Where is my data stored?

All data stays on your local machine. ScreamingCAT is a desktop application — nothing is sent to external servers (except when you explicitly use Google integrations with your own API credentials).

### Can I contribute to the project?

Yes. ScreamingCAT is open-source. Contributions, bug reports, and feature requests are welcome on GitHub.
