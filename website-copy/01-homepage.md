## Key Benefits Section

### Headline: Everything You Need to Audit Any Website

**Crawl Your Entire Site in Minutes**
Multi-threaded Rust engine with configurable speed, depth, and concurrency. Crawl thousands of URLs without breaking a sweat.

**Detect 60+ SEO Issues Automatically**
From broken links and missing H1s to mixed content warnings and structured data errors. Every issue is categorized by severity so you know what to fix first.

**Render JavaScript-Heavy Pages**
Built-in headless Chrome support means you see your pages the way Google sees them — AJAX content, SPAs, and all.

**Connect Google Tools You Already Use**
Pull in data from PageSpeed Insights, Google Search Console, and GA4 directly into your crawl results. No plugins needed.

**Export and Share Your Findings**
CSV, Excel, and XML sitemap exports. Save project files to revisit later. Compare snapshots to track changes over time.

**Visualize Site Structure**
Interactive crawl tree, site graph, and depth charts help you understand how your site is linked — and where the problems are.

## How It Works

### Headline: Three Steps to a Complete Site Audit

**1. Enter Your URL**
Paste any website URL and configure your crawl settings — depth, speed, user agent, URL filters, and more.

**2. Crawl and Analyze**
ScreamingCAT visits every page, follows every link, and extracts SEO data: titles, descriptions, headers, canonicals, structured data, response codes, and dozens more data points.

**3. Review and Fix**
Browse your results in a fast, filterable table. See issues sorted by priority. Export reports. Compare snapshots over time.
